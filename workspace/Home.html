Market Hog 2k18

June 1st, 2018 - September 19th, 2018

Picture taken on August 21st, 2018

Mamas is a cross bred swine, that as of August 28th, 2018 weighs in at 192 pounds. My goal is to have her at the very least 215 pounds by fair time. I've been feeding her CSS feed, vitamin B Comlex, gogurt, calves milk, fat, steamed rolled oat groats, lean maximizer, and champion drive. I plan to sell my hog to buyer number 9 for about $8 a pound as well as collect my sponsorship money. Other than making money, I did this project to have a eye opening experience.

Poultry 2k18

August 2nd, 2018 - Sep 19th, 2018

Picture taken on August 15th, 2018

This project has taught me what it is like to be a mom, becasue I am. I've raised these chicks since they were 1 day old. I've fed them, watered them, cleaned after them, etc. I donated 3 of them to Isis due to a tragic death of her market hog, I also sold 3 to Danny so he can enter the fair as an exibitor. On this project I will not make nearly the same amount of money as with the hog, but I did it because I really wanted to know what it was like to be resposible for the life of another live animal.

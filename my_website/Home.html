Mamas loved her belly rubs

Mamas is a cross bred swine, that as of September 21st, 2018 weighs in at 237 pounds. She is fed two meals per day which consist of CSS Grower feed, a variety of supplaments, all mixed with water. I plan to sell my hog to buyer number 9 for about $8 a pound as well as collect my sponsorship money. This project has taught me what it is like to be a mom, becasue I am. I've raised these chicks since they were 1 day old. I've fed them, watered them, cleaned after them, etc. I donated 3 of them to Isis due to a unfortunate death of her market hog, I also sold 3 to Danny so he can enter the fair as an exibitor.
